## Equipment
- [ ] Steamer
- [ ] Bowl
- [ ] Spoon

## Ingredients
- [ ] Steamed rice flour
- [ ] Sweet bean powder
- [ ] Water
- [ ] Sugar
- [ ] Salt

## Directions
1. Combine the rice flour, sweet bean powder, water, sugar, and salt in a bowl and mix well.
2. Place the mixture in a steamer and steam for 15 minutes.
3. Remove the injeolmi from the steamer and let it cool.
4. Serve and enjoy!
