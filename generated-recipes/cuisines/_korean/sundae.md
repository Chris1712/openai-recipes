---

## Equipment
- [ ] Bowl
- [ ] Spoon
- [ ] Frying pan
- [ ] Tongs

## Ingredients
- [ ] Pig's blood
- [ ] Rice
- [ ] Vegetables
- [ ] Spicy dipping sauce

## Directions
1. Combine pig's blood, rice, and vegetables in a bowl and mix well.
2.Shape mixture into small sausages.
3. Fry sausages in a pan over medium heat until browned.
4. Serve with a spicy dipping sauce.
