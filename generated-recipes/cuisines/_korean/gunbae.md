## Equipment
- [ ] Glasses
- [ ] Soju
- [ ] Beer
- [ ] Kimchi
- [ ] Rice
- [ ] Noodles
- [ ] Vegetables
- [ ] Meat

## Ingredients
- [ ] Soju
- [ ] Beer
- [ ] Kimchi
- [ ] Rice
- [ ] Noodles
- [ ] Vegetables
- [ ] Meat

## Directions
1. Pour soju and beer into glasses.

2. Add kimchi and rice to the glasses.

3. Add noodles and vegetables to the glasses.

4. Add meat to the glasses.

5. Enjoy your gunbae!
