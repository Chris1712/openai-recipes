## Equipment
- [ ] Tea kettle or pot
- [ ] Strainer
- [ ] Tea cup or mug

## Ingredients
- [ ] Chamomile flowers
- [ ] Water
- [ ] Honey (optional)

## Directions
1. Bring water to a boil.
2. Remove from heat and add chamomile flowers.
3. Steep for 5 minutes.
4. Strain and sweeten with honey, if desired.
