Ingredients:
1 egg

1 tbsp soy sauce

1 tbsp mirin

1 tbsp dashi

1 tbsp sugar

Instructions:

1. In a bowl, whisk together egg, soy sauce, mirin, dashi and sugar.

2. Pour the mixture into a small saucepan and cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until thickened.

3. Serve immediately over steamed rice.
