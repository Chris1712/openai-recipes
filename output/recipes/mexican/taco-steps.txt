1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Place the tortillas on a lightly greased baking sheet.
3. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 5 minutes, or until crisp.
4. In a skillet over medium heat, cook the beef until browned. Drain any excess fat.
5. Place the beef, lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, sour cream, guacamole, and pico de gallo in the tortillas, and fold to enclose.
