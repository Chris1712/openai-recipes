1. In a large pot, combine the beef stomach or tripe, chili peppers, garlic, onions, oregano, lime juice, salt, and water.

2. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 2-3 hours.

3. Remove the cooked beef stomach or tripe from the pot and cut into small pieces.

4. Return the beef stomach or tripe pieces to the pot and continue cooking for another hour.

5. Serve the menudo hot, with tortillas or bread.
