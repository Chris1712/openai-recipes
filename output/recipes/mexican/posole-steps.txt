1. Rinse and soak the hominy overnight.
2. In a large pot, cook the pork shoulder until browned.
3. Add the garlic, onion, chili peppers, oregano, cumin, salt, and pepper.
4. Add the hominy and enough water to cover.
5. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 2-3 hours.
6. Serve with shredded cabbage, lime wedges, and tostadas.
