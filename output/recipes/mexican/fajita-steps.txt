1. Preheat a grill, or heat a heavy skillet on the stove over medium-high heat.

2. Season your chicken, steak, or shrimp with salt, pepper, and any other desired spices.

3. Grill or cook your chicken, steak, or shrimp until cooked through.

4. Slice your chicken, steak, or shrimp into thin strips.

5. Slice your peppers and onions into thin strips.

6. Warm your tortillas on the grill or in the skillet.

7. Assemble your fajitas by adding chicken, steak, or shrimp strips, peppers and onions, salsa, guacamole, and cheese to your tortillas. Enjoy!
