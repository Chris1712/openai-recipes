1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add tortillas and cook until lightly browned, about 1 minute per side.
3. Spread enchilada sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
4. Roll up tortillas and place in the dish.
5. Top with cheese, sour cream, green onions, and diced tomatoes.
6. Bake until cheese is melted and bubbly, about 15 minutes.
