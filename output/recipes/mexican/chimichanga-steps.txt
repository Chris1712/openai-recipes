1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
2. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef with the onion and green pepper.
3. Add the tomato sauce, chili powder, cumin, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and oregano.
4. Add water and simmer for 10 minutes.
5. Meanwhile, heat the tortillas in the oven for about 5 minutes.
6. To assemble the chimichangas, place a layer of refried beans and cheese on each tortilla, then top with the ground beef mixture.
7. Roll up the tortillas and place in a baking dish.
8. Pour the remaining tomato sauce over the chimichangas.
9. Bake for 20-25 minutes. Serve with sour cream, salsa, and guacamole.
