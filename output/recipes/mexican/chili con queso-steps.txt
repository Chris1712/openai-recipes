1. Cut the Velveeta into small cubes.
2. Pour the Ro-Tel tomatoes into a blender or food processor and blend until smooth.
3. Pour the blended tomatoes into a saucepan and add the Velveeta cubes.
4. Stir over low heat until the Velveeta is melted.
5. Add chili powder, garlic powder, onion powder, cumin, salt, and pepper to taste.
6. Serve with tortilla chips.
