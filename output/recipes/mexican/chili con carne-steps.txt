1. In a large pot, brown the ground beef over medium heat.
2. Add the chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, salt, and pepper, and stir to combine.
3. Add the onion and tomato sauce, and stir to combine.
4. Add the water and beans, and stir to combine.
5. Bring the chili to a simmer, and cook for 10 minutes.
6. Add the cheese, and stir to combine.
7. Serve with sour cream.
