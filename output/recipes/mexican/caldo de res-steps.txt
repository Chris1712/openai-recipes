1. In a large pot over medium heat, cook the beef stew meat until browned.
2. Add the onion, celery, carrot, bell pepper, garlic, oregano, cumin, salt, pepper, and bay leaf. Stir to combine.
3. Add the diced tomatoes, enchilada sauce, and beef broth. Bring to a boil.
4. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 1 to 2 hours, or until the vegetables are tender.
5. Add the corn, black beans, and cilantro. Simmer for 10 minutes.
6. Serve with lime wedges.
