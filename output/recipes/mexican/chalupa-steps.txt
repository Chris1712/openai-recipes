1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees.
2. Spread a layer of refried beans on each tortilla.
3. Top each tortilla with cheese and then chopped lettuce and tomatoes.
4. Roll up each tortilla and place on a baking sheet.
5. Bake for 10-15 minutes, until tortillas are crispy.
6. Serve with sour cream and a chili pepper on top.
