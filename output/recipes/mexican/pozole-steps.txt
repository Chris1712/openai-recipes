1. In a large pot, combine the pork shoulder, hominy, chili peppers, tomatoes, onions, garlic, cilantro, salt, and pepper.
2. Fill the pot with enough water to cover the ingredients.
3. Bring the mixture to a boil over high heat.
4. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 2 to 3 hours, or until the pork is cooked through.
5. Remove the cooked pork from the pot and shred it into bite-sized pieces.
6. Return the pork to the pot and ladle the pozole into bowls.
7. Serve with your favorite toppings, such as sour cream, radishes, and lime wedges.
