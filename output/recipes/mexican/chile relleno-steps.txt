1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
2. Cut poblano pepper in half lengthwise and remove seeds.
3. Beat egg in a bowl.
4. In a separate bowl, mix flour and milk.
5. Dip pepper halves in the egg, then coat with the flour mixture.
6. Place peppers on a baking sheet and bake for 15 minutes.
7. Remove from oven and fill each pepper half with cheese.
8. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
9. Bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until cheese is melted.
