Ingredients:
1 cup jaggery

1 cup gram flour

2 tablespoons clarified butter

1 teaspoon green cardamom powder

1/4 cup chopped nuts (almonds, pistachios, cashews)

Instructions:

1. Melt the jaggery in a pan over low heat.

2. Add the gram flour and mix well.

3. Add the clarified butter and mix well.

4. Add the cardamom powder and mix well.

5. Add the chopped nuts and mix well.

6. Remove from the heat and let cool slightly.

7. Shape into small balls and serve.
