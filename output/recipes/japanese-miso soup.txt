Ingredients:
1 cup (236 ml) dashi

1/4 cup (59 ml) miso

1/2 cup (118 ml) tofu, cubed

1 scallion, thinly sliced

Directions:

1. Pour the dashi into a pot and bring to a simmer.

2. Add the miso and stir until dissolved.

3. Add the tofu and scallion and simmer for a few more minutes.

4. Serve hot.
