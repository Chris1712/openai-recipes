Ingredients:
1/2 lb. flat rice noodles

1/4 cup dark soy sauce

1/4 cup oyster sauce

1 tablespoon sugar

1 tablespoon chili garlic sauce

1/4 cup vegetable oil

1 red bell pepper, julienned

1 green bell pepper, julienned

1/2 onion, sliced

3 cloves garlic, minced

1/4 cup Thai basil leaves

1/2 cup chicken broth

1/2 lb. chicken, thinly sliced

1/4 cup Chinese rice wine or dry sherry

1 tablespoon cornstarch

Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

1. Soak noodles in warm water for 20 minutes. Drain and set aside.

2. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, oyster sauce, sugar, and chili garlic sauce. Set aside.

3. Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat. Add bell peppers, onion, and garlic. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.

4. Add noodles and sauce mixture. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.

5. Add chicken broth and chicken
