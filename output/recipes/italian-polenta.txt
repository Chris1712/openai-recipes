Ingredients:
1 cup polenta

4 cups water

1 teaspoon salt

1/4 cup olive oil

1/2 cup Parmesan cheese

Directions:

1. Bring the water to a boil in a large pot.

2. Add the salt and polenta, and stir to combine.

3. Reduce the heat to low and cook the polenta for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

4. Stir in the olive oil and Parmesan cheese, and serve.
