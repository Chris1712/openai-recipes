Ingredients:
1 lb potatoes, peeled and diced

1 lb cauliflower, cut into florets

2 tsp cumin seeds

1 tsp turmeric powder

1/2 tsp cayenne pepper

2 tbsp vegetable oil

1 tsp salt

1/2 cup water

1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro leaves

Directions:

1. Heat the oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add the cumin seeds and fry until they start to pop.

2. Add the potatoes, cauliflower, turmeric, cayenne, and salt. Stir well to coat the vegetables with the spices.

3. Add the water and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low and simmer, covered, for 20 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.

4. Stir in the cilantro and serve hot.
