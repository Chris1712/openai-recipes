Ingredients:
1 pound spaghetti

1/2 cup olive oil

4 cloves garlic, minced

1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes

1/4 cup chopped parsley

1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Salt and black pepper to taste

Directions:

1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of boiling water according to package instructions.

2. Meanwhile, heat the olive oil in a small skillet over medium heat.

3. Add the garlic and red pepper flakes and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.

4. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the skillet with the garlic oil.

5. Add the parsley, Parmesan cheese, salt and black pepper and toss to combine.

6. Serve immediately.
