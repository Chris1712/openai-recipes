Ingredients:
1 lb flank steak

1/2 onion, sliced

1/2 red pepper, sliced

1/2 green pepper, sliced

1/4 cup olive oil

1/4 cup lime juice

1 tsp chili powder

1 tsp garlic powder

1 tsp cumin

1/4 tsp salt

1/4 tsp black pepper

Instructions:

1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.

2. In a large bowl, combine steak, onion, red and green pepper, olive oil, lime juice, chili powder, garlic powder, cumin, salt, and black pepper.

3. Mix well and let marinate for at least 30 minutes.

4. Grill steak and vegetables for about 10 minutes, or until steak is cooked to your liking.

5. Serve steak and vegetables on warm tortillas and enjoy!
