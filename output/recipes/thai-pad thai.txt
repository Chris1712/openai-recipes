Ingredients:
1/2 package rice noodles

1/4 cup tamarind juice

1/4 cup fish sauce

1/4 cup brown sugar

1 tablespoon vegetable oil

3 cloves garlic, minced

1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes

1/2 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined

1/4 cup chopped peanuts

1/4 cup chopped green onions

1 lime, cut into wedges

Directions:

1. Soak rice noodles in warm water for 30 minutes.

2. In a small bowl, whisk together tamarind juice, fish sauce, brown sugar, and vegetable oil.

3. In a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat, stir-fry garlic and red pepper flakes in vegetable oil for 30 seconds.

4. Add shrimp and cook until opaque, about 3 minutes.

5. Add soaked noodles and tamarind sauce mixture. Toss to combine.

6. Cook until noodles are tender, about 5 minutes.

7. Garnish with peanuts, green onions, and lime wedges. Serve immediately.
