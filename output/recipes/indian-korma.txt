Ingredients:
1 lb diced lamb

1 onion, chopped

2 cloves garlic, chopped

1 inch piece ginger, chopped

1 teaspoon cumin seeds

1 teaspoon coriander seeds

1 cinnamon stick

2 cardamom pods

1 bay leaf

1 teaspoon turmeric

1 teaspoon ground cumin

1 teaspoon ground coriander

1/2 teaspoon ground cloves

1 cup plain yogurt

1/2 cup coconut milk

2 tablespoons ghee or butter

1 tablespoon fresh cilantro leaves, chopped

Salt to taste

Directions:

1. Combine the lamb, onion, garlic, ginger, cumin seeds, coriander seeds, cinnamon stick, cardamom pods, bay leaf, turmeric, cumin, coriander, cloves, yogurt, coconut milk, ghee or butter, cilantro leaves and salt in a large saucepan over medium heat.

2. Bring to a simmer and cook until the lamb is tender and cooked through, about 1 hour.

3. Serve hot with steamed rice or naan bread.
