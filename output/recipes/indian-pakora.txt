Ingredients:
1 cup gram flour (besan)

1 tsp chilli powder

Â½ tsp salt

1 tsp cumin powder (jeera)

2 tbsp finely chopped coriander (dhania)

2 tbsp finely chopped onion

4-6 tbsp water

1 tbsp oil for frying

Method:

Sieve the gram flour and add all the dry ingredients to it. Mix well.

Add the chopped onion and coriander and mix again.

Add enough water to make a thick batter.

Heat oil in a kadai and when hot drop spoonfuls of batter into it.

Deep fry on medium heat till golden brown and crisp.

Drain on absorbent paper.

Serve hot with chutney or ketchup.
