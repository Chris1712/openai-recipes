Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour

1 teaspoon baking soda

1/2 teaspoon baking powder

1/4 teaspoon salt

1 cup yogurt

1/2 cup milk

1/4 cup ghee

1 cup sugar

1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder

1 teaspoon fennel seeds

oil for deep frying

Directions:

1. Sift together the flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt.

2. In a bowl, whisk together the yogurt, milk and ghee.

3. Add the flour mixture to the wet ingredients and mix until well blended.

4. Cover the batter and let it rest for 2 hours.

5. In a saucepan, heat the sugar, cardamom powder and fennel seeds over low heat until the sugar is melted and the mixture is fragrant.

6. Heat the oil in a deep fryer or kadhai.

7. To make the jalebi, pour the batter into a piping bag with a star tip.

8. Pipe the batter into the hot oil in a spiral shape.

9. Fry the jalebi until golden brown
