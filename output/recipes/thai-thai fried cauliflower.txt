Ingredients:
1 head cauliflower, cut into florets

1/2 cup all-purpose flour

1/2 cup cornstarch

1 teaspoon baking powder

1 teaspoon salt

1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper

1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

1/2 teaspoon onion powder

1 egg, beaten

1 cup water

1 cup vegetable oil for frying

Instructions:

1. In a large bowl, combine the flour, cornstarch, baking powder, salt, black pepper, cayenne pepper, garlic powder and onion powder.

2. In another bowl, whisk together the egg and water.

3. Dip the cauliflower florets in the egg mixture, then coat with the flour mixture.

4. In a large skillet, heat the oil over medium-high heat. Add the cauliflower and fry for 3 to 5 minutes, or until golden brown.

5. Drain on paper towels and serve hot.
