Ingredients:
1 lb (500 g) paneer, cubed

2 tablespoons ghee or oil

1 teaspoon cumin seeds

1 medium onion, chopped

2 teaspoons fresh ginger, grated

3 cloves garlic, minced

1 teaspoon ground coriander

1 teaspoon ground cumin

1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric

1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

1 (28 ounce / 796 ml) can diced tomatoes, drained

1/2 cup (120 ml) water

2 cups (500 ml) spinach leaves, chopped

1/2 cup (120 ml) heavy cream

Salt to taste

Directions:

1. In a large heavy pot or Dutch oven, heat the ghee or oil over medium heat. Add the cumin and fry until lightly toasted, about 1 minute.

2. Add the onion, ginger and garlic and fry until the onion is golden brown, about 10 minutes.

3. Stir in the coriander, cumin, turmeric and black pepper and fry for 1 more minute.

4. Add the tomatoes and water and bring to a boil.

5. Reduce the heat to low
