Ingredients:
1 cup split yellow lentils (chana dal)

1/2 cup red lentils (masoor dal)

1/2 cup green lentils (moong dal)

1/2 cup toor dal

1 onion, finely chopped

2 cloves garlic, finely chopped

1-inch piece ginger, finely chopped

2 green chilies, finely chopped

1 teaspoon turmeric powder

1 teaspoon cumin seeds

2 tablespoons ghee or oil

1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Salt to taste

Water as needed

Instructions:

1. Wash all the lentils thoroughly and soak in enough water for 30 minutes.

2. Drain and transfer to a pressure cooker. Add the onion, garlic, ginger, green chilies, turmeric powder, cumin seeds and ghee or oil.

3. Mix well and pressure cook for 3-4 whistles or until the lentils are cooked through.

4. Open the pressure cooker and mash the dal with a spoon or an immersion blender.
