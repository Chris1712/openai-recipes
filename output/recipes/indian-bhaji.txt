Ingredients:
1/2 lb fresh spinach leaves

1 tablespoon oil

1/4 teaspoon cumin seeds

1/4 teaspoon mustard seeds

1/4 teaspoon fenugreek seeds

1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder

1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder

1 teaspoon ground coriander

2 cloves garlic, finely chopped

1/2 cup gram flour

1/4 cup water

Salt to taste

Freshly ground black pepper to taste

1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice

2 tablespoons chopped fresh coriander leaves

Method:

Wash the spinach leaves thoroughly and drain

Heat the oil in a karahi or wok over a medium flame and when hot add the cumin, mustard, fenugreek and turmeric powders

Stir fry for a minute and then add the chilli powder, ground coriander and garlic

Again stir fry for a minute and then add the gram flour, mixing well

Add the water and mix to a smooth paste

Add the spinach leaves and salt and mix well

Cover with a lid and cook for 5 minutes
