Ingredients:
-1 lb of pork belly

-1 onion

-1 carrot

-4 cloves of garlic

-1 teaspoon of grated ginger

-1 tablespoon of vegetable oil

-6 cups of chicken broth

-1 cup of sake

-1/2 cup of soy sauce

-1/2 cup of mirin

-1 teaspoon of sugar

-1 lb of fresh ramen noodles

-4 eggs

-4 green onions

Instructions:

1. Cut the pork belly into 1-inch cubes.

2. Peel and chop the onion.

3. Peel and chop the carrot.

4. Peel and mince the garlic.

5. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat.

6. Add the pork belly and cook until browned.

7. Remove the pork with a slotted spoon and set aside.

8. Add the onion, carrot, garlic, and ginger to the pot.

9. Cook until the vegetables are softened.

10. Add the chicken broth, sake, soy sauce, mirin, and sugar.
