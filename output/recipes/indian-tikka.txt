Ingredients:
2 lbs lamb or chicken, cubed

1 cup plain yogurt

1/2 cup chickpea flour

1 teaspoon ground coriander

1 teaspoon ground cumin

1 teaspoon ground ginger

1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

1 tablespoon lemon juice

2 cloves garlic, minced

1 teaspoon salt

1/4 cup butter, melted

1/2 cup fresh cilantro leaves, chopped

Directions:

1. In a large bowl, combine the lamb or chicken, yogurt, chickpea flour, coriander, cumin, ginger, cinnamon, black pepper, lemon juice, garlic and salt. Mix well to combine.

2. Preheat a grill or broiler.

3. Thread the meat onto skewers. Grill or broil for 10 to 15 minutes, or until cooked through.

4. Brush with the melted butter and sprinkle with the cilantro leaves before serving.
