Ingredients:
1 whole chicken, cut into pieces

2 quarts of water

1 onion, chopped

1 garlic clove, minced

1 carrot, chopped

1 celery stalk, chopped

1/2 teaspoon of dried oregano

1/4 teaspoon of cumin

1/4 teaspoon of salt

1/4 teaspoon of black pepper

1/2 cup of uncooked white rice

1/4 cup of chopped fresh cilantro

Directions:

1. In a large pot, bring the water to a boil.

2. Add the chicken pieces, onion, garlic, carrot, celery, oregano, cumin, salt, and black pepper.

3. Return to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for 30 minutes.

4. Stir in the rice and cilantro, and cook until the rice is tender, about 10 minutes.

5. Ladle the soup into bowls and serve.
