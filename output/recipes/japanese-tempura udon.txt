Ingredients:
1 cup dashi
1 cup water
1/4 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup mirin
1 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon sugar
1/2 teaspoon monosodium glutamate
1 (8-ounce) can bonito flakes
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 cup cornstarch
10 to 12 cups udon
1/2 cup tempura batter mix
1 cup Tempura sauce

Directions:

1. In a large pot, bring the dashi, water, soy sauce, mirin, salt, sugar, and monosodium glutamate to a boil.

2. Add the bonito flakes and simmer for 5 minutes.

3. Remove from the heat and strain the broth.

4. In a large frying pan, heat the oil over medium heat.

5. In a small bowl, mix together the flour and cornstarch.

6. Dip the udon in the flour mixture, then the tempura batter mix, and carefully add to the hot oil.

7. Fry the udon for 2 to 3 minutes, or until golden brown.

8. Serve with the tempura
