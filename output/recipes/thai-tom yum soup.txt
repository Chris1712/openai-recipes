Ingredients:
1 lb. shrimp, peeled and deveined

1/2 lb. mushrooms, sliced

1 lemongrass stalk, bruised

1 galangal root, sliced

2 kaffir lime leaves

1 Thai chili, sliced

5 c. chicken stock

1/4 c. fish sauce

1/4 c. lime juice

1/2 c. cilantro, chopped

Instructions:

1. In a large pot, bring the chicken stock to a boil.

2. Add the lemongrass, galangal, kaffir lime leaves, and Thai chili.

3. Simmer for 10 minutes.

4. Add the shrimp, mushrooms, fish sauce, and lime juice.

5. Cook until the shrimp are cooked through, about 5 minutes.

6. Ladle into bowls and garnish with cilantro.
