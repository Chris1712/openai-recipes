Ingredients:
1 lb (450 g) fillet steak

1/4 cup (60 ml) soy sauce

1/4 cup (60 ml) mirin

1 tablespoon (15 ml) vegetable oil

1/2 teaspoon (2.5 ml) ground black pepper

1/2 teaspoon (2.5 ml) garlic powder

1/2 teaspoon (2.5 ml) onion powder

1/4 teaspoon (1.25 ml) cayenne pepper

1/4 teaspoon (1.25 ml) ground ginger

Directions:

1. Combine the soy sauce, mirin, oil, black pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper and ginger in a large resealable plastic bag. Add the steak and marinate for at least 2 hours, turning the bag occasionally.

2. Preheat a grill or grill pan over high heat.

3. Grill the steak for about 3 minutes per side for medium-rare. Let rest for 5 minutes before slicing into thin strips.

4. Serve with steamed rice and grilled vegetables.
