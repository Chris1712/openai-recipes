Ingredients:
1 lb. Thai eggplant, sliced thinly

2 tablespoons oil

2 cloves garlic, minced

1 small onion, sliced

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/4 teaspoon black pepper

1/4 teaspoon sugar

1/2 cup chicken broth

2 tablespoons fish sauce

2 tablespoons oyster sauce

1/4 cup fresh basil leaves

Directions:

In a wok or large frying pan, heat oil over medium-high heat. Add garlic and onion and stir-fry for 1 minute. Add eggplant and stir-fry for 3 minutes. Add salt, pepper, sugar, chicken broth, fish sauce and oyster sauce. Bring to a boil and cook for 2 minutes. Add basil leaves and cook for 1 more minute. Serve hot.
