Ingredients:
1 pound sushi rice

1 cup Japanese rice vinegar

1/4 cup sugar

1 teaspoon salt

1/4 teaspoon wasabi paste

4 sheets nori seaweed

1/4 cup pickled ginger

1/2 cucumber, peeled and seeded

1 avocado, peeled and pitted

1/4 pound imitation crabmeat

1/4 cup mayonnaise

1 tablespoon sriracha sauce

Directions:

1. Cook the rice according to package instructions.

2. In a small bowl, whisk together the rice vinegar, sugar, salt and wasabi paste until the sugar has dissolved.

3. Once the rice is cooked, transfer it to a large bowl and pour the vinegar mixture over top. Gently stir the rice until it is evenly coated.

4. To assemble the sushi, lay a sheet of nori seaweed on a bamboo sushi mat. Spread a layer of rice over the nori, leaving a 1-inch border at the top.

5. Arrange a layer of pickled ginger, cucumber, avocado, crabmeat and mayonnaise in the center of the rice.
