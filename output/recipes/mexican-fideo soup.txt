Ingredients:
1 lb. fideo pasta

1 tbsp. olive oil

1 white onion, diced

4 cloves garlic, minced

8 cups chicken or beef broth

1 (14.5 oz) can diced tomatoes, undrained

1 (4 oz can diced green chiles, undrained)

1 tsp. chili powder

1 tsp. cumin

1/2 tsp. salt

1/4 tsp. black pepper

1/2 lb. boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces

1/2 cup frozen corn

1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Instructions:

1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.

2. Add the broth, tomatoes, green chiles, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer.

3. Add the chicken and corn and cook until the chicken is cooked through, about 5 minutes.

4. Stir in the cilantro and serve.
