Ingredients:
1/2 cup milk or heavy cream

1 tablespoon ghee

1 cup all-purpose flour

1 teaspoon baking powder

1/4 teaspoon baking soda

1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder

1/4 cup sugar

1/4 cup water

Oil for deep frying

Directions:

1. In a small bowl, combine milk and ghee. Stir well and set aside.

2. In a large bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda and cardamom powder.

3. Add sugar and milk mixture to the flour mixture. Stir until well blended.

4. Gradually add water to the mixture and knead into a soft dough.

5. Divide the dough into small balls.

6. Heat oil in a deep fryer or large saucepan.

7. Fry the balls in hot oil until golden brown.

8. Remove from the oil and drain on paper towels.

9. Serve warm or at room temperature.
