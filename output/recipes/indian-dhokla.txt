Ingredients:
1 cup rice

1 cup lentils (urad dal)

2 teaspoons baking powder

1 teaspoon salt

1 teaspoon turmeric powder

1 tablespoon sugar

1 tablespoon grated ginger

Water as needed

For the Tempering:

1 tablespoon oil

1 teaspoon mustard seeds

1 teaspoon cumin seeds

1 teaspoon fenugreek seeds

1 teaspoon asafoetida

1 teaspoon grated ginger

1 green chili, sliced

2 tablespoons chopped coriander leaves

Directions:

1. Soak the rice and lentils together in water for at least 4 hours.

2. Drain and grind to a smooth paste.

3. Add the baking powder, salt, turmeric, sugar and ginger and mix well.

4. Add water as needed to make a thick batter.

5. Grease a steaming tray and pour the batter into it.

6. Steam for 15 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

7. Let it cool slightly and then cut into squares.

8. For the tempering, heat the oil in a pan
