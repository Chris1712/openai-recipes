Ingredients:
4 fillets of white fish (cod, halibut or haddock)

1/3 cup soy sauce

1/3 cup mirin

1/3 cup sake

1/4 cup sugar

1/2 teaspoon grated ginger

1 garlic clove, minced

1 green onion, thinly sliced

1 teaspoon vegetable oil

Instructions:

1. Combine soy sauce, mirin, sake, sugar, ginger, garlic and green onion in a bowl.

2. Place the fish fillets in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them.

3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 2 hours.

4. Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.

5. Brush the fish with oil and grill for 2 to 3 minutes per side, or until cooked through.

6. Serve immediately.
