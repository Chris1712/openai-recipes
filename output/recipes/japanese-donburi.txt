Ingredients:
1 cup uncooked sushi rice

1 cup dashi or chicken broth

2 tablespoons soy sauce

1 tablespoon sugar

1 teaspoon salt

1/2 teaspoon mirin

1/2 teaspoon sake

1 (8-ounce) can tuna, drained

1/4 cup pickled ginger

1/4 cup chopped green onions

Directions:

1. Rinse the rice in a strainer under cold water until the water runs clear.

2. In a medium saucepan, bring the dashi or chicken broth to a boil.

3. Add the rice and stir to combine.

4. Cover and reduce the heat to low.

5. Simmer for 18 minutes, or until the rice is tender and cooked through.

6. Stir in the soy sauce, sugar, salt, mirin, and sake.

7. Gently fold in the tuna, pickled ginger, and green onions.

8. Divide the rice evenly among 4 bowls.
