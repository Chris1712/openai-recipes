Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

1 cup sugar

1 teaspoon baking powder

1/2 teaspoon salt

3 eggs

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

1/2 cup butter, melted

1/2 cup chopped almonds

Directions:

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a baking sheet.

2. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. In another bowl, whisk together eggs and vanilla. Stir in butter. Add to flour mixture, stirring just until moistened. Stir in almonds.

3. Divide dough in half. On prepared baking sheet, shape each half into a 12-inch-long, 2-inch-wide log.

4. Bake for 25 minutes, or until firm to the touch. Remove from oven, and let cool on baking sheet for 10 minutes.

5. Reduce oven temperature to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).

6. Using a serrated knife, cut each log diagonally into 1/2-inch-thick slices. Place slices, cut side down, on baking sheet.
