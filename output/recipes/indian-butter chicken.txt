Ingredients:
1 kg chicken breast, cut into cubes

1 tsp garam masala powder

1 tsp turmeric powder

1 tsp coriander powder

1 tsp cumin powder

2 tsp salt

1 cup curd (yogurt)

2 tbsp butter

2 cloves garlic, chopped

1 inch ginger, chopped

1 onion, chopped

1 green chili, chopped

1 tomato, chopped

1 bay leaf

1 cinnamon stick

2 cardamom pods

1 tsp kasoori methi (dried fenugreek leaves)

2 tbsp fresh cream

1 tbsp ghee (clarified butter)

2 tbsp chopped cilantro leaves

Method:

1. In a bowl, mix together chicken cubes, garam masala, turmeric, coriander, cumin and salt.

2. Add curd and mix well. Marinate for at least 1 hour.

3. In a pan, melt butter and fry garlic, ginger, onion and green chili until golden brown.

4. Add chicken and marinade. Cook for 5 minutes.
