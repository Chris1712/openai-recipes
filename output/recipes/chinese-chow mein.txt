Ingredients:
1 lb lean pork, trimmed and thinly sliced

1 lb chow mein noodles

1/4 cup soy sauce

1/4 cup dry sherry

1/4 cup vegetable oil

2 cloves garlic, minced

1/2 teaspoon ground ginger

1/4 teaspoon black pepper

1/2 cup chicken broth

1/4 cup cornstarch

2 tablespoons water

1/4 cup green onions, thinly sliced

Directions:

1. In a large bowl, combine pork, soy sauce, sherry, 1 tablespoon of the oil, garlic, ginger and pepper; let stand for 15 minutes.

2. In a large pot of boiling water, cook noodles for 3 to 4 minutes or until tender but firm. Drain and rinse with cold water; set aside.

3. In a wok or large skillet, heat remaining oil over high heat. Add pork mixture and chicken broth. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly.

4. In a small bowl, combine cornstarch and water; stir into pork mixture. Cook for 2 minutes or until sauce is thickened.

5. Add noodles and green onions; toss to combine.
