Ingredients:
For the chenna (milk curd):

2 litres whole milk

8 tablespoons lemon juice

For the malai (cream):

2 litres whole milk

1 teaspoon green cardamom (elaichi) powder

For the rabri (reduced milk):

2 litres whole milk

1 cup sugar

1/4 teaspoon green cardamom (elaichi) powder

For the syrup:

1 cup sugar

1 cup water

1 teaspoon rose water

Directions:

1. Pour the milk into a large saucepan and place over a medium heat.

2. Slowly bring the milk to the boil, stirring occasionally to prevent it from scorching.

3. When the milk has reached boiling point, remove from the heat and add the lemon juice.

4. Stir the milk gently until it curdles.

5. Line a large sieve with a clean muslin cloth and place over a bowl.

6. Pour the curdled milk into the sieve and allow it to drain for 30 minutes.
