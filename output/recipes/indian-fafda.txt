Ingredients:
1 kg gram flour

1/2 tsp baking soda

1/4 tsp turmeric powder

1/2 tsp asafoetida

Salt to taste

Water as required

Oil for deep frying

Method:

Sift the flour, baking soda, turmeric, asafoetida and salt together. Add enough water to form a stiff dough. Take small balls of the dough and roll out into thin sheets.

Heat the oil in a kadai and deep fry the sheets till golden brown. Drain on paper towels and serve hot with chutney of your choice.
