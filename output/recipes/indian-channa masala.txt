Ingredients:
1 lb dried chickpeas (kabuli channa)

1 tsp turmeric powder

1 tsp salt

1 tbsp garam masala powder

1 tbsp ground coriander

1 tbsp ground cumin

1 onion, chopped

2 cloves garlic, chopped

1â€³ piece ginger, chopped

3 green chillies, slit lengthwise

1 tbsp tomato puree

1 tbsp ghee or oil

1 cup water

2 tbsp fresh coriander leaves, chopped

Directions:

1. Soak the chickpeas in enough water to cover them for at least 8 hours or overnight. Drain and set aside.

2. In a large pot, combine the chickpeas, turmeric, salt, garam masala, ground coriander, cumin and onion.

3. Add enough water to cover the chickpeas and bring to a boil.

4. Reduce the heat and simmer for 1 hour or until the chickpeas are tender.

5. Add the garlic, ginger, green chillies, tomato puree and ghee or oil.

6. Simmer for another 5 minutes.
