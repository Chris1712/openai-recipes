Ingredients:
1 lb. lean pork, trimmed and cut into 1-inch cubes

1 (15 oz) can hominy, drained and rinsed

1 large onion, chopped

1 green bell pepper, chopped

4 cloves garlic, minced

2 tablespoons chili powder

1 teaspoon cumin

1 teaspoon dried oregano

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/4 teaspoon black pepper

6 cups chicken broth

1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Directions:

1. In a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat, combine the pork, hominy, onion, bell pepper, garlic, chili powder, cumin, oregano, salt, and black pepper.

2. Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a boil.

3. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 1 to 2 hours, or until the pork is cooked through.

4. Stir in the cilantro and serve.
