Ingredients:
1 cup whole wheat flour

1/2 teaspoon salt

1 tablespoon oil

1/2 cup water

Directions:

1. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl and mix well.

2. Knead dough until it is smooth, about 5 minutes.

3. Divide dough into 8 equal pieces.

4. On a lightly floured surface, roll each piece of dough into a thin circle.

5. Heat a griddle or skillet over medium heat.

6. Place roti on the griddle and cook for 1-2 minutes per side, or until golden brown.

7. Serve warm with your favorite dipping sauce.
