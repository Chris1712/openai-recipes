Ingredients:
1 tablespoon oil

1 teaspoon cumin seeds

2-3 green onions, thinly sliced

2 teaspoons minced garlic

2 teaspoons minced ginger

1-2 tablespoons green curry paste

1 can (14 oz) coconut milk

1-2 tablespoons fish sauce

1 tablespoon brown sugar

1-2 tablespoons fresh lime juice

1-2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro

1-2 tablespoons chopped fresh Thai basil

1-2 cups cooked chicken, shrimp, or tofu (optional)

1-2 cups vegetables (optional)

Instructions:

Heat the oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat. Add the cumin and fry until lightly toasted, about 1 minute.

Add the green onions, garlic, and ginger and fry for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

Stir in the curry paste and fry for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

Add the coconut milk, fish sauce, brown sugar, and lime juice and bring to a simmer.

Add the cilantro and Thai basil and simmer for 5-10 minutes.

Add the chicken, shrimp, or tofu and vegetables and simmer for 5-10 minutes
