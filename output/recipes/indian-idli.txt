Ingredients:
1 cup rice

1 cup urad dal (black gram)

1 tsp salt

1/2 tsp baking soda

1 tsp cumin seeds

1/4 cup curd (yogurt)

Method:

1. Soak rice and urad dal in separate bowls for at least 3 hours.

2. Drain and grind the dal to a smooth paste.

3. Add salt and cumin seeds and grind again.

4. Add rice and grind to a smooth paste.

5. Add curd and baking soda and mix well.

6. Leave the batter to ferment for at least 8 hours.

7. Grease idli moulds with oil and pour batter into them.

8. Steam for 10 minutes or until idlis are cooked through.
