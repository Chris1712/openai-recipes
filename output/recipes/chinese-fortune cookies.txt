Ingredients:
1/2 teaspoon baking powder

1/2 cup all-purpose flour

3 tablespoons sugar

2 egg whites

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Instructions:

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

2. Sift together the baking powder, flour, and sugar.

3. In another bowl, whisk together the egg whites, vanilla, and vegetable oil.

4. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and stir until well blended.

5. Place spoonfuls of batter on a greased cookie sheet and bake for 8 minutes.

6. Remove cookies from oven and place them on a cooling rack.

7. Once they are cool, place a fortune in the center of each cookie.
