Ingredients:
1 pound fresh tuna, sliced into thin strips

1 teaspoon grated ginger

1 tablespoon soy sauce

1 tablespoon rice vinegar

1 teaspoon wasabi paste

1 teaspoon sesame oil

Instructions:

1. In a large bowl, combine tuna, ginger, soy sauce, rice vinegar, wasabi paste and sesame oil.

2. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 2 hours.

3. To serve, arrange tuna strips on a platter or individual plates. Serve with soy sauce and wasabi paste, if desired.
