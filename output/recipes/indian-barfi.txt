Ingredients:
1 cup milk

1 cup sugar

1 cup semolina (rava)

1/2 cup ghee

1 tsp cardamom powder

1/2 cup chopped nuts (almonds, cashews, pistachios)

Instructions:

1. Heat milk in a saucepan over medium heat.

2. Add sugar and stir until dissolved.

3. Add semolina and stir constantly until the mixture thickens and comes away from the sides of the pan.

4. Add ghee and cardamom powder and mix well.

5. Add chopped nuts and mix well.

6. Grease a plate with ghee and pour the mixture onto the plate.

7. Spread evenly and let cool.

8. Cut into squares or diamonds and serve.
