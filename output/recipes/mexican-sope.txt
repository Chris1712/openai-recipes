Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef

1 small onion, diced

1/2 tsp. garlic powder

1/2 tsp. chili powder

1 (15 oz) can refried beans

10 (6 inch) corn tortillas

1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese

1/2 cup salsa

1/4 cup sour cream

Directions:

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.

2. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, cook ground beef, onion, garlic powder, and chili powder until beef is browned. Drain excess fat.

3. Spread refried beans over each tortilla, then top with beef mixture, cheese, salsa, and sour cream.

4. Roll up tortillas and place on a baking sheet.

5. Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until tortillas are crispy.
