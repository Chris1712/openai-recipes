Ingredients:
1/2 kg urad dal (black gram)

2 tsp salt

1 tsp asafoetida (hing)

1/2 tsp baking soda

1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste

1 green chilli, chopped

4 tbsp coriander leaves, chopped

1 onion, chopped

2 tbsp ghee (clarified butter)

Method:

Soak the urad dal in water for 3 hours. Drain and grind it in a blender to a smooth paste.

Add salt, asafoetida, baking soda, ginger-garlic paste, green chilli, coriander leaves and onion and mix well.

Wet your hands and shape the mixture into small balls.

Deep fry the balls in ghee until golden brown.

Serve hot with chutney or ketchup.
