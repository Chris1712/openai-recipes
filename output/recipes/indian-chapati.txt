Ingredients:
1 cup whole wheat flour

1/4 teaspoon salt

1 tablespoon oil

1/2 cup water

Instructions:

1. Combine flour and salt in a bowl. Add oil and mix well.

2. Gradually add water and knead into a soft dough.

3. Divide dough into 10-12 equal parts and shape into balls.

4. Roll each ball into a thin circle.

5. Heat a griddle or tava over medium heat.

6. Cook chapatis on the hot griddle, flipping once, until golden brown on both sides.

7. Serve hot with butter or pickle.
