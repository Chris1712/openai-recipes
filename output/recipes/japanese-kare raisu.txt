Ingredients:
1 lb lean ground pork

1/2 lb chicken, boneless & skinless, cut into small pieces

1/2 lb shrimp, peeled & deveined

1 cup uncooked white rice

1 large onion, chopped

1 large carrot, peeled & grated

2 stalks celery, chopped

1/2 head cabbage, chopped

1 (14 oz) can diced tomatoes

1 (8 oz) can tomato sauce

1 cup chicken broth

1/4 cup soy sauce

1 tbsp sugar

1 tbsp curry powder

1 tsp salt

1/4 tsp black pepper

1/2 cup green peas, frozen

Directions:

In a large pot or Dutch oven, brown the ground pork over medium-high heat. Drain off any excess fat.

Add the chicken, shrimp, rice, onion, carrot, celery, cabbage, tomatoes, tomato sauce, chicken broth, soy sauce, sugar, curry powder, salt and black pepper to the pot. Stir well.

Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer, covered, for 30 minutes, or until the rice is cooked through
