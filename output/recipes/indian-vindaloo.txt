Ingredients:
3 tablespoons of cooking oil

1 teaspoon of cumin seeds

2 medium onions, finely chopped

2 teaspoons of ginger, finely chopped

2 teaspoons of garlic, finely chopped

2 teaspoons of ground coriander

1 teaspoon of ground cumin

1 teaspoon of ground turmeric

1 teaspoon of chilli powder

2 teaspoons of white wine vinegar

2 bay leaves

2 whole cloves

1 cinnamon stick

2 tablespoons of tomato paste

2 cups of water

salt to taste

4-6 chicken breasts, boneless and skinless, cut into cubes

Method:

Heat the oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add the cumin seeds and fry until lightly browned. Add the onions, ginger and garlic and fry until the onions are golden brown. Add the coriander, cumin, turmeric, chilli powder and vinegar and fry for 1 more minute. Add the bay leaves, cloves, cinnamon stick, tomato paste and water and salt to taste, bring to a boil, reduce the heat and simmer for 30 minutes. Add the chicken and simmer for another 30 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through.
