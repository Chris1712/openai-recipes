Ingredients:
1 lb. chicken breasts

1 lb. beef marrow

1 onion

1 carrot

1 celery stalk

1 parsley sprig

1 bay leaf

1 clove garlic

2 quarts water

Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

1. In a large pot, combine all ingredients except salt and pepper.

2. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to a simmer.

3. Cook for 2 hours, skimming the surface occasionally.

4. Remove from heat and strain through a fine mesh sieve.

5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

6. Serve hot or cold.
