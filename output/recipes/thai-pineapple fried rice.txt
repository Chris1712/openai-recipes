Ingredients:
1 cup white rice

1/2 pineapple, cut into small pieces

1/4 cup green onions, chopped

1 tablespoon oil

2 tablespoons fish sauce

2 tablespoons soy sauce

1/4 teaspoon black pepper

1/2 cup cashews, chopped

1 egg, beaten

Instructions:

1. Cook rice according to package directions.

2. In a large skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat.

3. Add pineapple, green onions, fish sauce, soy sauce and black pepper.

4. Cook for 4 minutes, stirring occasionally.

5. Add cooked rice and cashews.

6. Cook for 2 minutes, stirring constantly.

7. Push rice mixture to one side of the skillet.

8. Add beaten egg to the other side of the skillet.

9. Cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly.

10. Stir egg and rice together.

11. Cook for 1 more minute.

12. Serve immediately.
