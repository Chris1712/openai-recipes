Ingredients:
3 cups Dashi

1/4 cup soy sauce

1 tablespoon sugar

1/4 cup mirin

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/2 pound soba noodles

1 sheet nori

1/4 cup green onions

1 cup shiitake mushrooms

1 cup chicken breast

1 egg

Instructions:

1. In a pot, bring dashi to a boil.

2. Add soy sauce, sugar, mirin, and salt.

3. Cook noodles in the dashi mixture according to package instructions.

4. Divide noodles into four bowls.

5. Top with nori, green onions, shiitake mushrooms, chicken, and egg.
