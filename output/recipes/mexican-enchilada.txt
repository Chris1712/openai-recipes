Ingredients:
-10-12 corn tortillas
-1-2 cups of your favorite enchilada sauce
-1-2 cups of shredded chicken or beef
-1 cup of shredded cheese
-1/2 cup of chopped onion

Instructions:

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.

2. In a large skillet, heat the tortillas over medium heat until soft.

3. Assemble the enchiladas by spooning the chicken or beef onto each tortilla, then topping with cheese and chopped onion.

4. Roll up the tortillas and place them seam side down in a baking dish.

5. Pour the enchilada sauce over the top and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
