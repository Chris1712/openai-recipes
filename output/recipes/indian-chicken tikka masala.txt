Ingredients:
1 lb chicken, cubed

1 onion, chopped

1 green chili, chopped

1 teaspoon cumin seeds

1 teaspoon coriander seeds

1 teaspoon ground ginger

1 teaspoon ground cumin

1 teaspoon ground coriander

1/2 teaspoon ground cardamom

1/2 teaspoon ground cloves

1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes

1 cup plain yogurt

1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro

Directions:

1. In a large bowl, combine chicken, onion, green chili, cumin seeds, coriander seeds, ginger, cumin, coriander, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, black pepper, tomatoes, yogurt, and cilantro.

2. Mix well and marinate for at least 2 hours, preferably overnight.

3. Preheat a grill or broiler.

4. Grill or broil chicken until cooked through, about 5 minutes per side.

5. Serve with rice and naan bread.
