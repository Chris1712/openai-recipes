Ingredients:
1 pound (450 grams) shrimp, peeled and deveined

1/2 cup (120 ml) all-purpose flour

1/2 cup (120 ml) cornstarch

1 teaspoon baking powder

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/2 teaspoon baking soda

1 egg, beaten

1 cup (240 ml) cold water

vegetable oil for frying

Directions:

1. In a large bowl, combine the flour, cornstarch, baking powder, salt and baking soda.

2. In another bowl, whisk together the egg and water.

3. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and whisk until smooth.

4. Heat the oil in a large saucepan or deep fryer to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

5. Dip the shrimp in the batter and carefully add to the hot oil.

6. Fry the shrimp until they are golden brown and cooked through, about 2 minutes.

7. Drain on paper towels and serve hot with dipping sauce.
