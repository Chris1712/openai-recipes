Ingredients:
1 tablespoon cooking oil

1 teaspoon cumin seeds

1 medium onion

2 teaspoons ginger-garlic paste

2-3 green chillies

1 teaspoon turmeric powder

1 teaspoon chilli powder

1 teaspoon coriander powder

1 tablespoon tomato paste

2 cups water

Salt to taste

2-3 tablespoons chopped fresh coriander leaves

Directions:

1. Heat the oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.

2. Add the cumin seeds and fry until they begin to change colour.

3. Add the onion and fry until golden brown.

4. Add the ginger-garlic paste and fry for a further minute.

5. Add the green chillies, turmeric powder, chilli powder and coriander powder and fry for a further minute.

6. Add the tomato paste and fry for a further minute.

7. Add the water and salt and bring to a boil.

8. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 10 minutes.

9. Add the chopped fresh coriander leaves and simmer for a further 5 minutes.
