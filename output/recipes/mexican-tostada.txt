Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef

1 small onion, diced

1 (15 oz) can refried beans

1 (10 oz) can red enchilada sauce

8 (6-inch) corn tortillas

1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Toppings:

Sour cream

Diced tomatoes

Sliced jalapenos

Directions:

Preheat oven to 350 degrees.

In a large skillet, cook beef and onion over medium-high heat until beef is browned. Drain fat and stir in refried beans and enchilada sauce.

Place tortillas on a large baking sheet. Spread beef mixture evenly over tortillas. Sprinkle with cheese.

Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until cheese is melted and tortillas are crispy.

 Serve with sour cream, diced tomatoes, and sliced jalapenos, if desired.
