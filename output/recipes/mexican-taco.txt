Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef

1 tablespoon chili powder

1 teaspoon cumin

1 teaspoon garlic powder

1 teaspoon salt

1/2 teaspoon black pepper

1/2 cup water

1 (15 ounce) can refried beans

8 (6 inch) corn tortillas

1/2 cup salsa

1/2 cup sour cream

Directions:

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

2. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, cook beef until browned. Drain fat, and stir in chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, salt, pepper, and water. Simmer for 10 minutes.

3. Spread refried beans over tortillas, and top with beef mixture. Roll up tortillas, and place in a 9x13 inch baking dish.

4. Pour salsa over the top, and spread sour cream over the salsa.

5. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes, or until tortillas are crispy.
