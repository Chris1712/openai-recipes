Ingredients:
1 pound pork shoulder

1 onion, chopped

3 cloves garlic, minced

1 teaspoon cumin

1 teaspoon chili powder

1/2 teaspoon oregano

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/4 teaspoon black pepper

1 (15-ounce) can hominy, drained and rinsed

1 (14.5-ounce) can diced tomatoes, undrained

1 (4-ounce can diced green chiles, undrained)

1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Directions:

1. Combine the first 8 ingredients in a slow cooker. Cover with lid, and cook on LOW for 6 hours or until the pork is tender.

2. Remove the pork from the slow cooker; shred with a fork, and return to the slow cooker. Add hominy and next 3 ingredients; cover with lid, and cook on LOW for 30 minutes or until thoroughly heated. Stir in cilantro.
