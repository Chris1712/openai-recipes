Ingredients:
1 large poblano pepper

1 egg

1/4 cup all-purpose flour

1/2 cup milk

1/4 cup grated cotija cheese

1/4 teaspoon baking powder

1/4 teaspoon salt

1 tablespoon vegetable oil

1/2 cup salsa

Directions:

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

2. Roast pepper on a baking sheet for 20 minutes, or until blackened. Place in a paper bag and let steam for 10 minutes. Peel off skin and remove seeds.

3. Beat egg in a bowl. Add flour, milk, cheese, baking powder, and salt; mix well.

4. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Stuff pepper with batter. Fry pepper in hot oil for 2 minutes per side, or until golden brown.

5. Serve pepper with salsa.
