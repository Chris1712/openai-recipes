Ingredients:
1 cup plain flour (maida)

1/2 teaspoon salt

1 tablespoon ghee

2 tablespoons milk

Water as required

Method:

1. Sift flour with salt in a bowl.

2. Rub in ghee then add milk and knead to a soft dough using water as required.

3. Allow to rest for 5 minutes.

4. Divide dough into 8-10 portions and roll out each a little.

5. Cook on a griddle (tava) on both sides, using a little extra ghee if required.
