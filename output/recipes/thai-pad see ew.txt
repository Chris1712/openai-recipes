Ingredients:
1/2 lb. wide rice noodles

1/4 cup oyster sauce

1 1/2 tablespoons dark soy sauce

1 tablespoon sugar

1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

2 tablespoons vegetable oil

2 cloves garlic, minced

1/2 lb. chicken breast, thinly sliced

1/2 lb. Chinese broccoli, trimmed and cut into 2-inch pieces

1/4 cup chicken broth

1/4 cup fresh Thai basil leaves

Directions:

1. Soak the noodles in warm water for 20 minutes. Drain and set aside.

2. In a small bowl, whisk together the oyster sauce, soy sauce, sugar and pepper.

3. Heat a wok or large skillet over high heat. Add the oil and swirl to coat. Add the garlic and chicken and stir-fry for 2 minutes.

4. Add the noodles and broccoli and stir-fry for 2 minutes.

5. Add the oyster sauce mixture and chicken broth and bring to a boil.

6. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 2 minutes.

7. Stir in the Thai basil leaves and serve.
