Ingredients:
1 cup rice flour

1 cup urad dal flour

1 teaspoon salt

1 teaspoon baking soda

1 tablespoon oil

1/2 cup curd

Water as required

Method:

Mix together all the ingredients in a bowl, adding just enough water to make a thick batter.

Heat a non-stick tava (griddle) and pour a ladleful of batter onto it.

Spread it out evenly and cook on both sides till golden brown.

Serve hot with chutney or sambar.
