Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil

1 yellow onion, diced

1 tablespoon minced garlic

1 tablespoon chili powder

1 teaspoon ground cumin

1 teaspoon dried oregano

1/2 teaspoon salt

4 cups chicken broth

2 (15-ounce) cans diced tomatoes, undrained

1 (15-ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained

1/2 cup chopped cilantro

6 (6-inch) corn tortillas, cut into strips

1/2 cup sour cream

1 lime, cut into wedges

Directions:

1. Heat the oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add the onion, garlic, chili powder, cumin, oregano, and salt. Cook, stirring, until the onion is softened, about 5 minutes.

2. Add the chicken broth, tomatoes, black beans, and cilantro. Bring to a simmer and cook for 10 minutes.

3. Add the tortilla strips and cook for 5 minutes.

4. Ladle the soup into bowls and top with the sour cream and lime wedges.
