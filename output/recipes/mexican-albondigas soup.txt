Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef

1/2 cup uncooked rice

1/2 onion, chopped

1 egg

1 clove garlic, minced

1 tsp. salt

1/4 tsp. black pepper

1/2 tsp. cumin

1 (14.5 oz) can diced tomatoes, undrained

1 (10 oz) can enchilada sauce

4 cups water

Directions:

1. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, rice, onion, egg, garlic, salt, pepper, and cumin. Mix well.

2. Shape mixture into 1-inch balls.

3. In a large pot over medium heat, combine tomatoes, enchilada sauce, and water. Bring to a boil.

4. Add meatballs to the pot. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 30 minutes, or until meatballs are cooked through.
