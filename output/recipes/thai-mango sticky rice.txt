Ingredients:
1 cup glutinous rice

1 cup coconut milk

1/4 cup sugar

1/2 teaspoon salt

1 ripe mango, sliced

Instructions:

1. Wash the rice in several changes of water, then soak in water for at least 2 hours.

2. Drain the rice and place in a saucepan with the coconut milk, sugar and salt.

3. Bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring frequently.

4. Reduce the heat to low and simmer, covered, for 15 minutes.

5. Remove from the heat and let sit for 5 minutes, then fluff with a fork.

6. Serve the rice in bowls, topped with mango slices.
