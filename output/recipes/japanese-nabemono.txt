Ingredients:
4 small chicken breasts, skinned and boned

1/2 cup dashi

1/4 cup soy sauce

1/4 cup mirin

1/4 cup sake

1 small daikon radish, peeled and julienned

1/2 carrot, peeled and julienned

1 small leek, white part only, julienned

20 small shiitake mushrooms, stems removed

8 oz firm tofu, cut into small cubes

1/4 cup chopped scallions

1/4 cup chopped cilantro

Instructions:

1. Combine the dashi, soy sauce, mirin, and sake in a small bowl and set aside.

2. Place the chicken in the bottom of a large, heavy pot or Dutch oven.

3. Add the daikon, carrot, leek, shiitake mushrooms, tofu, scallions, and cilantro.

4. Pour the dashi mixture over the top.

5. Place the pot over high heat and bring to a boil.

6. Reduce the heat to low and simmer, covered, for 30 minutes.
