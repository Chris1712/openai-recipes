Ingredients:
1 cup urad dal (skinless black lentils)

1/2 cup rice

1 tsp salt

Water

Instructions:

1. Soak the urad dal and rice in water overnight.

2. In the morning, grind the soaked dal and rice into a smooth paste using a little water.

3. Add salt and mix well.

4. Heat a non-stick tava (griddle) and pour a ladleful of the batter onto it.

5. Spread it out evenly into a thin circle.

6. Cook on one side till crisp and golden brown.

7. Remove from the tava and serve hot with chutney or sambar.
