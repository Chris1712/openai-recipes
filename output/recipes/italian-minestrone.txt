Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil

1 onion, diced

2 carrots, diced

2 celery stalks, diced

1 potato, diced

1 zucchini, diced

1/2 head cabbage, diced

1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes, undrained

1 can (15 ounces) kidney beans, undrained

4 cups chicken broth

1 teaspoon salt

1/4 teaspoon black pepper

1/2 cup uncooked elbow macaroni

1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Directions:

In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the onion, carrots, celery, potato, zucchini, and cabbage. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Stir in the tomatoes, beans, chicken broth, salt, and black pepper. Bring to a boil.

Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 30 minutes.

Stir in the macaroni and parsley. Simmer for an additional 10 minutes, or until the macaroni is cooked.

Serve with Parmesan cheese, if desired.
