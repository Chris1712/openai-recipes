Ingredients:
1 pound (450g) pork loin, trimmed and cut into 4 equal portions

1/2 cup (120ml) all-purpose flour

2 eggs, beaten

1 cup (240ml) panko bread crumbs

vegetable oil, for frying

salt and pepper, to taste

For the sauce:

1/2 cup (120ml) ketchup

1/4 cup (60ml) Worcestershire sauce

1 tablespoon (15ml) soy sauce

1/2 teaspoon (2.5ml) garlic powder

Instructions:

1. Preheat a large skillet over medium-high heat and add enough oil to coat the bottom.

2. Season the pork loin pieces with salt and pepper. Place the flour in a shallow dish, the eggs in another shallow dish, and the panko in a third shallow dish.

3. Dredge each piece of pork in the flour, then the eggs, and then the panko, making sure to coat evenly.
