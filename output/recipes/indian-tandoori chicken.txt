Ingredients:
2 lb chicken drumsticks or thighs

1 cup plain yogurt

1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated

1 tablespoon fresh garlic, minced

2 teaspoons ground cumin

1 teaspoon ground coriander

1 teaspoon ground paprika

1 teaspoon salt

1 teaspoon ground black pepper

1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice

1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Instructions:

1. Wash the chicken and pat dry with paper towels.

2. In a large bowl, combine the yogurt, ginger, garlic, cumin, coriander, paprika, salt, pepper, and lemon juice.

3. Add the chicken to the bowl and coat evenly with the yogurt mixture.

4. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight.

5. Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.

6. Brush the grill or pan with oil.

7. Grill the chicken for about 10 minutes per side, or until cooked through.

8. Serve hot.
