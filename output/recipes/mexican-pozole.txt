Ingredients:
2 lbs. pork shoulder

1 lb. hominy

1 onion

1 green bell pepper

2 cloves garlic

1 tsp. oregano

1 tsp. cumin

Salt and pepper

6 cups water

Instructions:

1. In a large pot, combine the pork shoulder, hominy, onion, green bell pepper, garlic, oregano, cumin, salt and pepper.

2. Add water and bring to a boil.

3. Reduce heat and simmer for 2-3 hours or until the pork is cooked through.

4. Serve with chopped onions, cilantro, lime wedges and radishes.
