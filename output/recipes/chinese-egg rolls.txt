Ingredients:
1 lb. pork

1/2 lb. shrimp

1/4 lb. mushrooms

1/4 lb. bamboo shoots

1/4 lb. water chestnuts

1 onion

2 tablespoons soy sauce

1 tablespoon cornstarch

1 teaspoon salt

1/4 teaspoon pepper

10 oz. wonton skins

1 egg

vegetable oil

Instructions:

1. Combine the pork, shrimp, mushrooms, bamboo shoots, water chestnuts, onion, soy sauce, cornstarch, salt and pepper in a large bowl and mix well.

2. Place a wonton skin on a work surface and spoon 1 tablespoon of the filling in the center.

3. Lightly beat the egg and brush the edges of the wonton skin with it.

4. Fold the bottom corner up over the filling, then fold the two side corners in and roll the egg roll up tightly.

5. Repeat with the remaining wonton skins and filling.

6. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat.

7. Add the egg rolls and cook until golden brown and crisp, about 3 minutes per side.
