Ingredients:
1 litre Milk

1/2 cup Curd (yogurt)

1/2 tsp Lime juice

2 tblsp All-purpose flour (maida)

2 tblsp Rice flour

1 cup Sugar

1/2 tsp Cardamom seeds

1/4 tsp Nutmeg powder

1/4 cup Rose water

1/2 cup White poppy seeds

1/4 cup Water

Directions:

1. Boil the milk in a heavy-based pan over medium heat.

2. When it comes to a boil, remove from the heat and add the curd and lime juice. Stir gently to mix.

3. Cover and set aside for 5 minutes to allow the milk to curdle.

4. Strain the mixture through a muslin-lined sieve into another heavy-based pan.

5. Add the all-purpose flour, rice flour, sugar, cardamom seeds, nutmeg powder and rose water.

6. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and comes away from the sides of the pan.

7. Remove from the heat and stir
