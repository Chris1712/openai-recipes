Ingredients:
1 egg

1 cup dashi

1 teaspoon soy sauce

1 teaspoon mirin

1/4 teaspoon salt

1/2 cup chopped chicken

1/4 cup chopped shrimp

1/4 cup chopped mushrooms

1/4 cup chopped bamboo shoots

1 green onion, chopped

Instructions:

1. Beat egg in a bowl.

2. Mix dashi, soy sauce, mirin and salt in a pot and bring to a boil.

3. Add egg and stir gently.

4. Add chicken, shrimp, mushrooms, bamboo shoots and green onion.

5. Cover and simmer for 3 minutes.

6. Ladle into bowls and serve.
