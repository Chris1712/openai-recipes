Ingredients:
1 lb. Velveeta cheese, cubed

1 (10 oz) can diced tomatoes with green chilies, undrained

1/2 cup milk

1 tablespoon chili powder

Directions:

In a medium saucepan over medium heat, combine the cheese, tomatoes, milk, and chili powder. Cook, stirring frequently, until the cheese is melted and the mixture is smooth. Serve immediately.
