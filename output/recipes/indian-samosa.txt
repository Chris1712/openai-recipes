Ingredients:
3 cups of all-purpose flour

1 teaspoon salt

1 teaspoon baking powder

1/2 cup clarified butter, melted

1 cup water

2 tablespoons clarified butter, melted

1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds

1 medium onion, chopped

1 teaspoon ground ginger

1 teaspoon ground coriander

1/4 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper

1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric

1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice

1 (15 ounce) can chickpeas, drained and mashed

2 tablespoons fresh cilantro leaves, chopped

1/2 cup raisins

1/2 cup minced dried apricots

1/2 cup minced pitted dates

1/2 cup chopped cashews

1/2 cup chopped almonds

1/2 cup chopped pistachios

1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:

1. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, salt and baking powder. Stir in melted butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Gradually add water, stirring constantly until mixture forms a soft dough.

2. On a lightly floured surface, knead dough for about 10 minutes.
