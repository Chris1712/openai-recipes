Ingredients:
1 cup uncooked rice

1/4 cup chopped onion

1 clove garlic, minced

1/4 cup chopped green bell pepper

1/4 cup chopped red bell pepper

1/4 cup chopped celery

1/4 cup chopped carrot

1 teaspoon olive oil

1/4 teaspoon salt

1/4 teaspoon black pepper

1/4 cup chopped fresh basil

1/4 cup chopped peanuts

1/4 cup canned pineapple chunks

1/4 cup raisins

1/4 cup shredded coconut

Directions:

1. Cook rice according to package directions.

2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Add onion, garlic, bell peppers, celery, and carrot. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 5 minutes.

3. Add cooked rice, salt, and black pepper. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 5 minutes.

4. Stir in basil, peanuts, pineapple, raisins, and coconut. Cook for 1 minute, or until heated through.
