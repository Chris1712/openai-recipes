Ingredients:
-1 tablespoon vegetable oil

-1/2 onion, diced

-1 clove garlic, minced

-1/2 pound ground beef

-1/2 teaspoon chili powder

-1/4 teaspoon cumin

-1/4 teaspoon salt

-1/4 teaspoon black pepper

-1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chilies, undrained

-8 (6 inch) flour tortillas

-1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

-1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Directions:

1. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and cook until the onion is translucent.

2. Add the ground beef, chili powder, cumin, salt, and black pepper, and cook until the beef is browned.

3. Stir in the diced tomatoes with green chilies.

4. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

5. Place the tortillas on a baking sheet. Spread the beef mixture over the tortillas. Sprinkle with the Cheddar cheese and cilantro.

6. Bake in the preheated oven
