Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef

1 small onion, diced

1 green bell pepper, diced

1 (15 oz) can diced tomatoes

1 (15 oz) can red kidney beans

1 (15 oz) can black beans

1 tablespoon chili powder

1 teaspoon cumin

1 teaspoon garlic powder

10 (6-inch) flour tortillas

1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.

2. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, cook the ground beef, onion, and bell pepper until the beef is browned. Drain any excess fat.

3. Stir in the diced tomatoes, kidney beans, black beans, chili powder, cumin, and garlic powder.

4. To assemble the burritos, place about 1/2 cup of the beef mixture in the center of each tortilla, and top with 1 tablespoon of cheese.

5. Roll up the tortillas, and place them seam-side down in a 9x13 inch baking dish.

6. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the burritos are heated through and the cheese is melted
