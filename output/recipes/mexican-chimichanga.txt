Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef

1 small onion, chopped

1 green pepper, chopped

1 (15 oz) can diced tomatoes

1 (4 oz can diced green chilies)

1 tsp. chili powder

1 tsp. cumin

1/2 tsp. salt

1/4 tsp. black pepper

8 (10-inch) flour tortillas

1/2 cup (2 oz) shredded cheddar cheese

vegetable oil

Directions:

1. In a large skillet, over medium-high heat, cook the ground beef, onion, and green pepper until the beef is browned.

2. Stir in the tomatoes, green chilies, chili powder, cumin, salt, and black pepper.

3. Cook for 5 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.

4. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

5. To assemble the chimichangas, place 1/2 cup of the beef mixture in the center of each tortilla.

6. Sprinkle with 1 tablespoon of cheese.

7. Fold the bottom and top of the tortilla towards the center
