Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups masa harina

1 cup water

1 teaspoon salt

1 tablespoon lard or vegetable shortening

1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Instructions:

1. In a large bowl, combine masa harina, water, salt, and lard or shortening. Mix well.

2. On a lightly floured surface, knead dough for about 5 minutes.

3. Divide dough into 12 balls.

4. Using your thumb, make an indentation in the center of each ball.

5. Fill each indentation with 1 tablespoon of cheese.

6. Pinch dough closed.

7. On a lightly floured surface, flatten each ball into a 4-inch disk.

8. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook gorditas for about 2 minutes per side, or until golden brown.

9. Serve warm with your favorite salsa or toppings.
