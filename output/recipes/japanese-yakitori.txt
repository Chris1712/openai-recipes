Ingredients:
1 lb chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces

2 tbsp soy sauce

1 tbsp sake

1 tbsp mirin

2 tsp sugar

1/2 tsp salt

1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper

1/4 cup vegetable oil

1 bunch scallions, trimmed and cut into 1-inch pieces

1/2 cup chicken broth

1/4 cup sake

1/4 cup mirin

1/2 tsp salt

1 skewer per piece of chicken

Directions:

1. In a large bowl, combine chicken, soy sauce, sake, mirin, sugar, salt, and pepper. Mix well and set aside to marinate for at least 30 minutes.

2. In a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat, heat oil. Add chicken and cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until browned.

3. Add scallions, chicken broth, sake, mirin, and salt. Bring to a boil and cook for about 3 minutes, until sauce has thickened.
