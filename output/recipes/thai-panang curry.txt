Ingredients:
1 lb. chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces

1 red bell pepper, cut into bite-sized pieces

1 green bell pepper, cut into bite-sized pieces

1 (14 oz) can coconut milk

1/2 cup Panang curry paste

1 tablespoon fish sauce

1 tablespoon brown sugar

1 teaspoon ground ginger

1/2 teaspoon ground cumin

1/4 teaspoon ground cloves

1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon

1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

1 tablespoon vegetable oil

1/4 cup chopped peanuts

1/4 cup chopped cilantro

Directions:

1. In a large bowl, whisk together coconut milk, curry paste, fish sauce, brown sugar, ginger, cumin, cloves, cinnamon, and black pepper.

2. In a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat, heat oil. Add chicken and bell peppers and cook until chicken is cooked through and peppers are tender, about 5 minutes.

3. Add coconut milk mixture to the skillet and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 5 minutes.
