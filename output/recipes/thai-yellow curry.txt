Ingredients:
1 tablespoon yellow curry paste

1 tablespoon cooking oil

1 can (14 ounces) coconut milk

1 tablespoon fish sauce

1 teaspoon sugar

1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces

1 large red bell pepper, cut into bite-sized pieces

1 large carrot, cut into bite-sized pieces

1 large potato, cut into bite-sized pieces

1/2 cup frozen green peas

1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Instructions:

1. In a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat, cook curry paste in oil until fragrant, about 1 minute.

2. Add coconut milk, fish sauce and sugar; bring to a simmer.

3. Add chicken, bell pepper, carrot and potato; cook until chicken is cooked through and vegetables are tender, about 10 minutes.

4. Stir in peas and cilantro; cook for 1 minute. Serve over cooked rice.
