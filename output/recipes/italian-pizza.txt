Ingredients:
1 pound pizza dough

1/2 cup tomato sauce

1/2 cup grated mozzarella cheese

1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

1/2 teaspoon dried oregano

1/4 teaspoon garlic powder

1/4 teaspoon salt

1/4 teaspoon black pepper

1/2 cup thinly sliced pepperoni

Directions:

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).

2. Roll pizza dough out onto a lightly floured surface. Spread tomato sauce over dough.

3. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, oregano, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper over sauce. Arrange pepperoni slices over cheese.

4. Bake pizza in the preheated oven for 15 minutes, or until crust is golden brown and cheese is melted and bubbly.
