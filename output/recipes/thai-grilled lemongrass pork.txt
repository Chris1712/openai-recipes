Ingredients:
6 pork chops

4 stalks lemongrass, trimmed and finely chopped

4 cloves garlic, finely chopped

2 tablespoons fish sauce

1 tablespoon light brown sugar

1 teaspoon ground black pepper

1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Preparation:

1. Combine the lemongrass, garlic, fish sauce, sugar and pepper in a large resealable plastic bag. Add the pork chops and turn to coat. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight.

2. Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.

3. Brush the grill or grill pan with oil. Add the pork chops and grill for 5 to 7 minutes per side, or until cooked through.

4. Remove from the grill or grill pan and serve immediately.
