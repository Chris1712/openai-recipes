Ingredients:
1 lb chicken breast, cut into cubes

2 tbsp cooking oil

1/2 tsp salt

1/2 tsp black pepper

1/2 tsp MSG

1/4 cup soy sauce

1/4 cup cooking wine

1/4 cup sugar

1 tbsp cornstarch

1/2 cup peanuts

1/4 cup green onions, chopped

2 cloves garlic, minced

1 tbsp chili paste

1 tsp sesame oil

Directions:

1. Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken, salt, pepper, and MSG. Stir-fry for 3 to 5 minutes, or until chicken is no longer pink in the center.

2. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, cooking wine, sugar, and cornstarch. Add soy sauce mixture to the wok.

3. Add peanuts, green onions, garlic, chili paste, and sesame oil. Stir-fry for another 2 to 3 minutes, or until sauce is thickened and chicken is cooked through.

4. Serve with steamed rice.
