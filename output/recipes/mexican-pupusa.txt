Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups masa harina

1 1/2 cups water

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/4 teaspoon baking powder

1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions:

1. In a large bowl, combine the masa harina, water, salt, and baking powder. Mix well.

2. Add the mozzarella cheese and mix until well blended.

3. Divide the dough into 12 balls.

4. On a lightly floured surface, flatten each ball into a 4-inch circle.

5. Place 1/4 cup of the filling in the center of each circle.

6. Fold the dough over the filling and press the edges together to seal.

7. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the pupusas for about 5 minutes per side, or until golden brown.

8. Serve with salsa and sour cream.
