Ingredients:
1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into 1-inch pieces

1 tablespoon vegetable oil

1 tablespoon Thai red curry paste

1 can (13.5 ounces) unsweetened coconut milk

1/2 cup chicken broth

1 tablespoon fish sauce

1 teaspoon sugar

1 red bell pepper, seeded and cut into 1-inch pieces

1 green bell pepper, seeded and cut into 1-inch pieces

1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Instructions:

In a large skillet over medium-high heat, heat oil. Add chicken and curry paste and cook, stirring, until chicken is browned, about 3 minutes.

Stir in coconut milk, chicken broth, fish sauce and sugar. Bring to a simmer and cook until chicken is cooked through, about 5 minutes.

Stir in bell peppers and cilantro and cook until peppers are tender, about 3 minutes.

Serve over rice.
