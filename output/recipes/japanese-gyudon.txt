Ingredients:
1 lb. thinly sliced beef

1 onion, sliced

2 cups dashi or beef broth

1/4 cup soy sauce

1/4 cup mirin

1/2 teaspoon sugar

1/2 cup uncooked white rice

Instructions:

1. In a large pot or dutch oven, combine dashi or beef broth, soy sauce, mirin and sugar.

2. Add the beef and onion, and bring to a simmer.

3. Cover and cook until the beef is tender, about 30 minutes.

4. Add the rice, and continue cooking until the rice is cooked through, about 10 minutes.

5. Serve in bowls, and enjoy!
